## Goal
Make sources a real domain owner for ingestion, connector logic, and citations.

## Current repo cues
- `backend/app/services/source_services/`
- `backend/app/api/sources/`
- `backend/app/utils/citation_utils.py`
- `backend/app/utils/source_content_utils.py`

## Scope
- Create `backend/app/sources/` subpackages such as `ingest/`, `connectors/`, `citations/`, and any other source-owned homes needed.
- Move source ingestion/extraction orchestration into `sources/`.
- Move source-specific citation and source-content helpers into `sources/`.
- Define a small public surface for source upload/process/query flows.
- Keep file-format adapters in `platform/` and source rules in `sources/`.

## Out of scope
- Structured analysis migrations (covered separately).
- Changing extraction behavior.
- Changing vector search behavior.

## Deliverables
- Owned `sources/` package with clear subpackages.
- Updated imports from routes and domains.
- Source-owned citation/content helpers moved out of `utils/`.

## Acceptance criteria
- Source ingestion and citations are owned by `backend/app/sources/`.
- No source-specific helper remains in `backend/app/utils/` except temporary shims.
- The path makes source ownership obvious.

## Notes
The key split is domain rules in `sources/` and vendor/file adapters in `platform/`.
