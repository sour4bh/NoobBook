## Goal
Make marketing generation features live together as one conceptual neighborhood.

## Current repo cues
- `backend/app/api/studio/ads.py`
- `backend/app/api/studio/emails.py`
- `backend/app/api/studio/infographics.py`
- `backend/app/api/studio/marketing_strategies.py`
- `backend/app/api/studio/social_posts.py`

## Scope
- Create or complete `studio/marketing/ads/`, `email/`, `infographic/`, `marketing_strategy/`, and `social_post/`.
- Move route-facing services, generation logic, models, and prompt/tool assets into each item slice.
- Keep `studio/marketing/shared/` tiny and real; do not create a wrapper farm.
- Update routes and imports to use the new public surfaces.

## Out of scope
- Moving design or document features.
- Behavioral changes to prompts or workflows.
- Global shared abstractions across all studio categories.

## Deliverables
- Marketing item slices.
- Updated imports and tests.
- A clear category-level ownership boundary.

## Acceptance criteria
- Marketing items can be navigated from `studio/marketing/` without chasing other top-level folders.
- No marketing feature remains owned by a generic executor taxonomy.
- The directory tree expresses category containment clearly.

## Notes
This ticket is about semantic locality, not new abstractions.
