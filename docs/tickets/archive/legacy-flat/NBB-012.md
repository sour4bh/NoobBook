## Goal
Put design-generation items in one home and make the logo/brand boundary explicit.

## Current repo cues
- `backend/app/api/studio/components.py`
- `backend/app/api/studio/flow_diagrams.py`
- `backend/app/api/studio/websites.py`
- `backend/app/api/studio/wireframes.py`
- `backend/app/api/studio/logo_utils.py`
- `backend/app/api/brand/`

## Scope
- Create or complete `studio/design/component/`, `flow_diagram/`, `website/`, and `wireframe/`.
- Migrate design item logic, models, and assets into the owning slices.
- Make an explicit decision about `logo_utils.py`: either move it under `brand/` if it is brand-owned, or under `studio/design/shared/` if it is design-generation support.
- Document the decision so future contributors do not guess.

## Out of scope
- Learning/media items.
- Brand redesign work unrelated to ownership.
- Cross-category abstractions.

## Deliverables
- Design item slices.
- Documented logo/brand ownership decision.
- Updated imports and tests.

## Acceptance criteria
- Design items are co-located under `studio/design/`.
- Logo ownership is explicit in the path and docs.
- No design item remains owned by a generic executor bucket.

## Notes
The logo decision matters because it will otherwise become a perpetual ambiguity point.
