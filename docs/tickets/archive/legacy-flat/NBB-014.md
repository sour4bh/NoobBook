## Goal
Give the smaller backend domains the same ownership clarity as the larger ones.

## Current repo cues
- `backend/app/api/projects/`
- `backend/app/api/settings/`
- `backend/app/api/brand/`
- `backend/app/services/app_settings/`
- `backend/app/services/data_services/`

## Scope
- Move project-owned behavior into `backend/app/projects/`.
- Move settings-owned behavior into `backend/app/settings/`.
- Move brand-owned behavior into `backend/app/brand/`.
- Expose small public surfaces for each domain and update route imports accordingly.
- Keep generic data access helpers out of these domains unless they are truly shared and belong in `base/` or `platform/`.

## Out of scope
- Studio/logo ownership already decided elsewhere.
- Major data-model redesign.
- Cross-domain policy changes.

## Deliverables
- Owned packages for projects, settings, and brand.
- Updated imports from routes and services.
- Reduced reliance on generic buckets for smaller domains.

## Acceptance criteria
- A maintainer can find project/settings/brand behavior by opening the matching package.
- No smaller-domain behavior is stranded in generic service buckets without an explicit reason.
- Route imports point at domain-owned public surfaces.

## Notes
Do this after the major domains so the target layout and platform/auth boundaries are already stable.
